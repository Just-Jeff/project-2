Characters

Characters in Genshin Impact are unlocked through a gacha based system. This involves the player spending in-game currency at a chance to roll for them in what are called "Banners", featuring rare loot and characters. Several characters and items are unlocked through the game’s natural progression and are given to you for free during special events.

These "Banners" feature several characters or items as the main highlight of an event, allowing the player to roll for them at an increased chance compared to the normal odds of unlocking them. In-game currency can be either gained through natural gameplay progression, or bought through the online store to roll for the characters and items you want.

Every 10 rolls you spend in a "Banner", you are gaurenteed a 4-star item or character, with a 5-star item or character being gaurenteed at 50 rolls. You can check the odds for specific items and characters under each "Banner" in the details section of that said "Banner". Please visit the main site to see a comprehensive list of all the game's characters and legendary items.

Below is a short list of some unlockable characters and their corresponding bio for Genshin Impact:

The Traveler

The Traveler is the protagonist and a playable character in Genshin Impact.

Twin siblings from another world, the traveler and their twin, journey throughout the universe until one day they are ambushed by an Unknown God, capturing one of them, and leaving the other stranded on the strange world of Teyvat.

Noelle

Working as a maid at the Knights of Favonius headquarter’s, Noelle dreams of one day becoming a knight.

While she may seem like a timid girl, she posses incredible strength and defense capable of defeating even the most terrifying foes. Whether it be protecting the people of the land or cleaning up, Noelle is always ready to help those in need.

Beidou

Captain of the Crux, an armed fleet based in Liyue Harbor, Beidou is a ferocious pirate who sails the seas and cuts down anyone in her way.

Beidou draws lighting with her sword, and is said to be capable of cutting down mountains in her way. No matter what the seas throws at her, she’s sure to split them in two.

Diluc

Current owner of the Dawn Winery and a nobleman of high esteem, Diluc is a wealthy gentleman of Teyvat, who holds resentment towards the knights of Favonius.

While he appears apathetic about the affairs of the land, he protects it at night as the rumored “Darknight Hero”, striking with a burning zeal.
