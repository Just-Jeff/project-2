Gameplay

Each character has two unique combat skills: an elemental skill and an elemental burst. The elemental skill is an ability that can be used at any time, providing a cooldown period immediately after use. The elemental burst acts as a character’s ultimate ability, requiring the user to amass a sufficient level elemental energy by defeating enemies, or inflicting elemental statuses, before it can be used.

Each character has control over one of seven natural elements in the game being: Cryo, Dendro, Pyro, Hydro, Anemo, Electro and Geo. These correspond to: ice, nature, fire, water, air, electricity, and earth. These elements can interact with each other in a variety of different ways. For example, if a character uses a Hydro attack against a target, the enemy will be inflicted with the “Wet” status effect. If the enemy is hit with a Cryo attack, they will be inflicted with the “Cold” status.

These two statuses combine into the “Frozen” status, which temporarily prevents the enemy from performing any actions, allowing the player to deal bonus physical and elemental damage, causing the “Shatter” effect to the enemy.

Switching between characters during combat and executing elemental skills is vital to allow these elemental interactions to take place dealing massive damage with stunning effects. Certain elemental abilities may be required to solve puzzles within the overworld, and other elemental abilities may be naturally activated in the overworked, such as zapping enemies by who are standing in a puddle of water.
